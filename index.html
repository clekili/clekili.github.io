  • Fluid Simulator

    Fluid Simulator implements a physics simulator that visualizes the behavior of fluids in an interactive environment. The algorithm is based on the Navier-Stokes equations which define the fluid flow in physics. Mathematical methods used in the simulation include; Gauss-Seidel relaxation, Hodge decomposition and Poisson's equation.
